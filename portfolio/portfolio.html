About Me

Hello! I’m Biruk Belete, a dedicated video editor and translator with over 3 years of experience in the industry. My passion for storytelling drives my work, whether I'm crafting engaging videos or translating content to connect with diverse audiences. As a video editor, I specialize in creating promotional content, documentaries, short videos, and utilizing software like Capcut, Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro. I excel at transforming raw footage into compelling narratives that resonate with viewers. In addition to video editing, I am fluent in Amharic, English, and Spanish and have worked on various translation projects, ensuring the original message and tone are preserved. My background in translation has equipped me with the skills to deliver high-quality translations. I believe in the power of visual storytelling and language to connect people. If you’re looking for a passionate video editor or translator to bring your vision to life, I’d love to collaborate!
